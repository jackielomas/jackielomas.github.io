Utilities Page

City of Flora contains all necessary utilities within one bill. Notifying City of Flora of a move will take care of all disconnection needs for your residence. Below is a list of the different utilities that will be disconnected.
  1. Utilities
  2. Electric
  3. Trash
  4. Water
  5. Trash & Sewage
  6. Natural Gas

This information can be found at City of Flora.
