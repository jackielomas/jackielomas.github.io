Cleaning List

  1. Wipe down all surfaces (walls, baseboards, molding, doors, etc)
  2. Wipe down all kitchen cabinets (inside, outside, & top) & drawers
  3. Ensure stove and oven are cleaned
  4. Clean all bathrooms (sink, mirrors, tub, etc.)
  5. Wipe down washer & dryer (inside & outside)
  6. Make sure air intake vent is cleaned and filter replaced
  7. Vaccum carpet and/or sweep & mop floors
  8. Ensure all belongings and trash are removed from location
  9. Check to ensure outside premises is acceptable (yard mowed if required, no trash in yard, etc.)

Repairs and Cleaning Page

You can always ask your landlord for a list of moving out repairs and a cleaning list to complete before leaving your rental place. Many larger rental companies will contain these lists in your lease agreement so you should always refer back to that before completing anything on your own.

Here is a list of common repairs to complete before moving out of a rental home:

  • Replace any cabinetry door knobs that may have been removed or broken off
  • Replace any light bulbs no longer working
  • Try and repair any scuffs in the walls
  • Attempt to repair any scratching or scuffs in wood flooring
  • Plaster any holes in walls from nails or other forms of hanging (check with landlord before completing this task)
